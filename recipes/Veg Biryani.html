Veg Biryani

Description

Vegetable Biryani is an aromatic dish made with basmati rice, mix veggies, herbs and biryani spices. You will love this for everything from busy night meals to holiday family dinners. Biryani is a celebration of exotic flavors, vibrant colours and pleasing aromas infused into a pot of goodness.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • half cup onion
  • 1 green chilli
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • half teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 small tomato
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 tablespoon oil or ghee
  • 2 tablespoon curd
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt for veggies + 1/4 teaspoon for water
  • 1/3 cup potatoes
  • 1/3 cup carrot
  • 1/3 cup green peas
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 inch cinnamon
  • 3 cloves
  • 3 green cardamoms
  • 1/2 teaspoon shahi jeera
  • 1 star anise (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Soak rice for atleast 20 mins.
  2. Chop all the veggies and coriander. Keep these aside as well.
  3. If using cauliflower keep them slightly larger
  4. Add oil to hot pressure cooker or heavy bottom pot.
  5. Next add bay leaf, star anise, shahi jeera, cloves, cardamoms and cinnamon saute for 1 to 2 mins
  6. Add sliced onions and slit green chillies. Fry them until the onions turn golden brown
  7. Next add ginger garlic paste and saute for about a minute or until the raw smell has gone off
  8. Add all the chopped vegetables and fry for about 2 minutes
  9. Next add tomatoes, 1/8 teaspoon salt, yogurt, coriander leaves, red chilli, turmeric and garam masala
  10. Mix all of these and fry on a high flame for about 2 to 3 mins
  11. Reduce the flame to low. Drain the water from the rice completely and spread it in a largely evenly over the veggies
  12. Measure the water to a separate bowl and add 1/4 tsp salt. Mix well.
  13. Pour the salted water across the sides or edges of the pot
  14. Mix everything up well
  15. If using a pressure cooker, cook for 1 whistle on a medium flame. If using a pot, cook covered on a low to medium flame until the water is absorbed and the biryani is fully cooked
  16. Sprinkle the lemon juice on top and serve :)
